About

Where research-based design meets real classrooms

I'm an instructional designer and Learning & Development specialist based in Leuven. My work sits at the intersection of evidence-informed educational design, student-centred guidance, and practical implementation — shaped by more than ten years of teaching, mentoring and career coaching.

At KU Leuven's Faculty of Economics and Business, I designed Flourish Abroad, an intercultural learning course for exchange students. I built its six complete learning modules end to end — defining learning outcomes, structuring the content, creating the digital activities and assessments, and delivering everything on the Toledo LMS.

I bring instructional design, learning coordination, stakeholder management and a data-informed approach to every project — and I care deeply about accessibility and belonging in how people learn.
